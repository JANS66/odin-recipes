Spicy Chickpea Tacos

Description

Spicy Chickpea Tacos are a vibrant, plant-based twist on taco night. The chickpeas are pan-roasted with bold spices until crispy, delivering a satisfying crunch and smoky flavor. Topped with fresh veggies and a cool dollop of sour cream, these tacos are quick, healthy, and endlessly customizable.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 small tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream or dairy-free alternative
  • Fresh lime wedges
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chickpeas and spices.
  2. Cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chickpeas are crispy and well coated.
  3. Warm tortillas and assemble with chickpeas, lettuce, onion, tomatoes, and a dollop of sour cream.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and a squeeze of lime.
