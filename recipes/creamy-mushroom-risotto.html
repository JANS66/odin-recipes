Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Description

Creamy Mushroom Risotto is a comforting Italian classic that turns humble ingredients into a rich, luxurious meal. Arborio rice slowly absorbs flavorful broth as it cooks, creating a velvety texture that's enhanced by sautéed mushrooms, butter, and Parmesan. It’s a cozy, meatless option perfect for dinner any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced (cremini or button)
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth, warm
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic; sauté until soft.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and brown slightly.
  3. Stir in Arborio rice and toast for 1-2 minutes. Add white wine and let it reduce.
  4. Gradually add warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously and letting the liquid absorb before adding more.
  5. Once the rice is creamy and tender (about 18-20 minutes), remove from heat.
  6. Stir in butter, Parmesan, and season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley.
